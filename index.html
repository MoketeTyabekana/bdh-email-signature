Botshabelo Digital Hub is an incubation hub focused on fostering innovative ICT solutions. Established in 2019 through partnerships with the Department of Small Business Development (DSBD) and others, it provides infrastructure, mentorship, and networking to help businesses succeed in the digital economy.

With a vision to create globally competitive enterprises, the hub supports both start-ups and established businesses, promoting entrepreneurship, collaboration, and innovation.
